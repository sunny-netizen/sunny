Cities and Road Networks

My masters dissertation revealed weak relationships between hierarchical structure in the road network of Europe and human geography.

I extracted OpenStreetMap data as a network graph to conduct percolation analysis, then modelled the nested relationships of percolation clusters as a hierarchical tree for phenetic analysis.

Climate Resilience and Sustainability

As a CivicSpark Fellow with the City of Culver City, I built capacity for the Ballona Creek Revitalization Project and a City Ban On Single-Use Plastics. I have since written about data and climate action in local government.

Joining the Advisory Council of Ballona Creek Renaissance, I created an Esri ArcGIS StoryMap for outreach and developed a map animation for an educational video.

Stars and Planets

Inspired by star maps, I created a map of exoplanets using data from the NASA Exoplanet Archive.

The same data source was used to create fictional worlds based on The Midnight Gospel.
